About Andree Chedid

Born Andree Saab in Cairo in 1920. She was of Libanaise, Egyptia, French nationality. She was honoured in her lifetime with Prix Goncourt de la poesie and in 2009 Grand Officier de la Legion d'honneur

Above all Andree Chedid was a humanist and her work centred on the human condition. She was strongly opposed to violence and war.

Andree celebrated life in her poetry but she also understood its precariousness.
